*Canon Keeper · Vol. I*

# **Where imagination takes a *body.***

---

This isn't a writing app. It's a **living studio** for the dreamers who were told their ideas were too big, too wild, too much.

**Novelists. Screenwriters. Worldbuilders. Movie-makers in their own heads.**

Zagafy is where what's inside you walks out onto the page — and starts talking back.

---

## ✦ The Manifesto ✦

> *Stop dreaming of being a writer. Become one.*

Every writer knows the moment. Chapter nineteen, three a.m., and you can't remember if your villain has a brother or a blade. The page stares back. The doubt creeps in. The story you were building starts to **slip**.

Zagafy is the place where that stops happening.

Your characters **answer you**. Your worlds remember themselves. Your stuck moments become sparks. The AI never writes the book for you — it writes *with* you, the way a best friend brainstorms at 3 a.m., handing you the match, never the fire.

You become the author. The screenwriter. The maker of the next thing people can't stop watching.

## 🕯 The Library

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **💬 Character Chat** | Your villain talks back. Your hero argues with you. Pull your cast out of the page and let them tell you what happens next. |
| **🔒 Canon Lock** | Every character, rule, and secret you've ever written — protected. Contradict yourself and Zagafy catches you before the reader does. |
| **🪶 Flow Mode** | No backspace. No delete. Just forward. A distraction-free writing temple where momentum glows behind every word. |
| **🧭 Writer's Block Diagnostics** | Afraid? Perfectionist? Empty? Zagafy asks first — then softens, sharpens, or steadies itself to meet you where you are. |
| **🎭 Heteronyms** | Slip into alter-ego voices when your own isn't coming. Write as the poet, the cynic, the child — then return home, richer. |
| **🧠 Story Brain** | A second mind watching for patterns, threads, and obsessions you can't see yourself. Your subconscious, rendered. |
| **🗺 Writing Map** | See the shape of your obsession over time. Heatmaps, rituals, sprints — every word you've ever written, rendered as landscape. |
| **🕊 Entry Rituals** | A breath. A quote. A pause. Writing is sacred, and your tools should treat it that way. Begin each session with a door, not a dashboard. |
